Left mouse - select any objects
Left click empty space - to deselect
A - to select all
Alt + A - to deselect all

Middle Mouse Button - to pan 3d spaces
Shift + Middle Mouse Button - to pan 2d spaces
View -> Frame Selected - to focus on the selected object
Scroll Wheel Button - Zoom in and Zoom out

Perspective Mode - realistic view of your 3d model
Orthograpic Mode - ignores all that, unrealistic view but makes model pixel perfect
